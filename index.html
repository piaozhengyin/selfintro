Determination of global and local characteristic properties for cleavage fracture assessment of high strength bolt steels

Industrial Research Project
Oct.2015 - Nov.2016
Steel Institute of RWTH Aachen

Keywords: master curve, Beremin model, FE simulation, cleavage fractrure

This work aims to determine characteristic properties of high strength bolt steels in the strength class 10.9 for cleavage fracture assessment. Fracture mechanics tests are conducted to determine the experimental Master Curves. Then numerical approaches are employed to derive the parameters of Beremin model from Master Curves. The results have been published:

Bo Wu, Benedikt Döbereiner, Zhengyin Piao, Björn Sabotke, Victoria Brinnel, Sebastian Münstermann, Determination of global and local characteristic properties for cleavage fracture assessment of high strength bolt steels, Materials Testing. 59(2017), 945-950.
